# Settings & Preferences

Control your account settings, privacy, and integrations here.

## Account

- Update your name, email, and plan. Manage billing and subscription from the Billing panel.

## Preferences

- Theme: toggle Light/Dark.
- Notification preferences: email, push, and marketing emails.

## Integrations

- Connect Gmail to enable email sync and automatic tracking of application threads.
- Revoke access at any time from this screen.
