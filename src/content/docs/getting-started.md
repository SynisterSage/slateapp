# Getting Started with SlateApp

Welcome to SlateApp. This guide helps you get up and running quickly.

## Create an account

1. Click **Sign up** and provide your email.
2. Verify your email address.

## Upload your first resume

Go to **Resumes → Upload Resume** and choose a PDF or DOCX file. We parse the file and extract structured fields.

## Connect Gmail (optional)

If you want to sync emails for job contacts and application notifications, connect Gmail in **Settings → Integrations**.

## Where to go next

- Dashboard: overview of applications and matches
- Jobs: search and apply to roles
- Support: open the `?` menu for help and FAQs
